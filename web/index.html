How it works

No hardware to buy if you already own an Ecowitt station. Just link it, let it log, and start exploring.

1

Link your Ecowitt station

Paste your Ecowitt application key, API key and station MAC. Your live readings — temperature, wind, rain, pressure and more — appear in seconds.

2

Watch it log automatically

The app records your readings on a schedule, building 12 months of history and trends you can scroll back through any time.

3

Compare, install and go

Add other locations to compare side by side, pull in your current location, then install the app to your home screen.

Frequently asked questions

Which weather stations are supported?
Personal Ecowitt stations. You connect using your own Ecowitt application key, API key and station MAC address — your data stays tied to your account.
Where does the history come from?
Once your station is linked, the app logs its readings on a schedule so you build up your own 12 months of history and trends. Forecasts for other locations come from Open-Meteo.
Do I need to install anything?
No. It runs in any modern browser. Because it's a PWA you can also add it to your phone or desktop home screen.
Can I track more than one location?
Yes. Alongside your own station you can add other locations to compare side by side, plus your current GPS location.
What does it cost?
There are no account fees to get started. You'll need your own Ecowitt station and a free Ecowitt API key; the app reads from that and from Open-Meteo.
